FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers

What's included in the 10-day free trial? ▾
The Starter plan free trial gives you full access to our document automation templates, email filtering setup, and PM task creation automation — no credit card required. After 10 days you can continue at $40/mo or cancel with no obligation.
What tools do you integrate with? ▾
We work with the tools your team already uses: Notion, Jira, Slack, Gmail, Outlook, Google Drive, Zapier, Make, HubSpot, Salesforce, and many more. If you're using it, we can likely automate around it.
What makes ZaraAI different from other automation agencies? ▾
Two things: security and depth. Jax brings 10+ years of U.S. Army Special Forces cybersecurity experience — we think about AI security in ways most automation shops don't. And Zara isn't just a brand name, it's an actual AI agent actively running client operations.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ▾
Yes, absolutely. Plans are flexible. You can start on Starter, scale to Growth as your needs grow, or work directly with us to build a custom engagement. Just reach out to info@thezaraai.com.
Is the scoping call onboarding mandatory for Growth and Scale? ▾
The scoping call is included by default but can be waived for experienced teams who already know what they need. It exists to make sure we set you up correctly — not to gatekeep. Contact us and we'll work it out.
How is my data handled? Is it secure? ▾
Security is core to everything we do — it's literally one of our services. We follow enterprise security practices, never store sensitive client data beyond what's needed, and can provide a full data handling overview on request.
