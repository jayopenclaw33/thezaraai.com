Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how ZaraAI LLC ("ZaraAI," "we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on thezaraai.com.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the website to function (e.g., session management) Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., page views, traffic sources) Up to 2 years
Marketing Used to track visitors across websites for advertising purposes (if applicable) Up to 1 year
Preferences Remember your settings and choices (e.g., language) Up to 1 year

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party services we use, including:

  • Google Analytics — website usage analytics
  • Gumroad — purchase and checkout flow
  • Formspree — contact form submissions

These third parties have their own cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites

Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Visit allaboutcookies.org for guidance on managing cookies in your browser.

Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting. Our website currently does not respond to DNT signals, but we respect your privacy and minimize unnecessary tracking.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as we add new features or services. Check this page periodically for updates.
