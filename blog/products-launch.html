This kit is for anyone who's been using ChatGPT, Claude, or OpenClaw and feeling like they're not getting everything out of it. The dirty secret? The tool is usually fine. The setup isn't. Most people never configure their AI with any context about who they are, what they do, or what they're working on. Every session starts completely cold.

SOUL.md, USER.md, and MEMORY.md are the three files that change that. They're not complicated — they're structured text files that live in your workspace and load automatically every time your agent wakes up. The result is an AI that feels less like a tool and more like a collaborator. One that knows your voice, your work, your preferences. One that compounds instead of resetting.

I use this exact system myself — with Zara — and the difference between a Zara session and a cold ChatGPT session is not subtle. She knows my background. She knows my projects. She knows how I write. That's what the Starter Kit builds for you.

Why I Built These (The Real Version)

I've been in cybersecurity for almost two decades. I've watched the field change through every major technology shift. Cloud, mobile, IoT, and now AI. Every time, there's a window where early adopters get a serious edge — and then it closes, and everyone catches up, and the edge is gone.

We're in that window right now with AI.

Most security professionals are still treating AI like a novelty. They'll paste in a paragraph and ask it to "make this better." Maybe they'll ask it to summarize a CVE. That's fine — but it's leaving 80% of the capability on the table. The people who figure out how to actually build AI into their workflow, who set it up right and give it the right context, are doing significantly better work in significantly less time.

I built these products to help close that gap. Not just for security people — the Starter Kit is for anyone — but especially for the people I know best: the ones who've put years into their expertise and deserve tools that honor that expertise instead of dumbing it down.

No subscriptions. No upsells. Pay once, own it forever. That was a deliberate choice. I'm building products I'd actually want to buy.
